About VoxMatch

Tap Start to find someone to talk to

What is VoxMatch?

VoxMatch is a free random voice chat website. Tap Start, and your browser is paired live, one-on-one, with a random stranger who's online right now — no app download, no sign-up, no profile to fill in. Talk by voice over WebRTC, or switch to text chat alongside the call.

How does the random matching work?

Matching is anonymous and unfiltered: there's no search, no swiping, no choosing who you get paired with. Your browser is given a random, anonymous identifier so the matchmaking and anti-abuse systems can work — not an account or anything tied to who you are. Don't like who you're talking to? Tap Skip and you're matched with someone new in seconds.

Is it really free, and is my call private?

Yes — VoxMatch has no paid tiers, ads gating the call, or required account. Voice audio travels directly between you and your matched partner over a peer-to-peer WebRTC connection and isn't recorded or stored on our servers. See the Privacy Policy for exactly what is and isn't stored.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to create an account?

No. There's no sign-up, username, or password. Just accept the terms and tap Start.

What happens if someone is abusive?

Every call has a Report button. Reporting enough times temporarily blocks an identity from matching with anyone else. See the Terms of Service for the full community guidelines.

Can I use VoxMatch on my phone?

Yes — VoxMatch runs in any modern mobile or desktop browser, no app install required.

Who is this for?

Anyone 18 or older looking for a quick, anonymous voice conversation with someone new. Read more on the About VoxMatch page.

What we collect

VoxMatch does not require an account, email address, name, or phone number. When you first load the site, our server issues your browser a random, anonymous identifier stored in a cookie (not something you choose or that identifies you personally). We also see your IP address as part of normal web traffic.

Voice calls and chat messages

Voice audio travels directly between you and your matched partner (WebRTC peer-to-peer) and is not recorded or stored on our servers. Text chat messages are relayed live to your current partner only and are not stored after the conversation ends.

Reports, bans, and rate limiting

If you report another user, we store your anonymous identifier, the reported identifier, and a timestamp for up to 7 days, to enforce our abuse policy. If an identifier accumulates enough reports, it is temporarily blocked from matching for 24 hours. We also briefly track request counts per identifier and IP address to prevent abuse (rate limiting).

Cookies

We set one functional cookie to remember your anonymous identifier (kept for up to 1 year, or until you clear your cookies). This cookie is required for the matching and anti-abuse system to work and is not used to build an advertising profile by us directly.

If this site displays ads (for example, via Google AdSense), Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve and personalize ads and measure their performance. You can learn more and manage your ad personalization settings at policies.google.com/technologies/ads and adssettings.google.com.

Children's privacy

VoxMatch is intended for users 18 and older only, gated by a consent checkbox before you can start a call. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

Data retention summary

  • Identity cookie: up to 1 year, or until cleared by you
  • Report records: up to 7 days
  • Ban records: 24 hours
  • Voice audio and chat messages: not stored

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update this page. Continued use of VoxMatch after a change means you accept the updated policy.

What this is

VoxMatch is a random voice-chat web app: click Start, and you're paired with a random other person currently online for a live, one-on-one voice call. It also includes optional text chat alongside the call, and a Skip button if you'd rather move on to someone new.

How matching works

Matching is anonymous and random — there's no profile, no search, no filtering by who you talk to. You don't need an account; we issue your browser an anonymous identifier so the matching and anti-abuse systems can work.

Safety and moderation

You must confirm you're 18 or older and agree to our Terms of Service before starting. Every call has a Report button: if someone is reported enough times, they're temporarily blocked from matching. See our Privacy Policy for what we store and for how long.
